Services

Solar panels maintenance requires trained & professional staff to ensure that panels are as clean as new and as efficient as possible; SOLARS not only ensure this, but also provide a full fledged maintenance service.

Solar Panel Cleaning

Your solar panels need to be exposed to sunlight in order to produce power. However, with high amounts of smog, dust, dirt or sand blowing around, solar panel cleaning becomes necessary.

Efficiency Check & Maintenance

It is generally recommended that you perform solar panel cleaning between two and four times per year. This helps ensure maximum productivity & minimum electricity bills.

Protective Barriers Fixing

Protective barriers offers a level of protection, limits the number of service and repair calls and provides an aesthetic advantage.

Inspection & Fixation

The best way to ensure your solar installation lasts as long as possible is installing it correctly to begin with and inspecting it thoroughly to head off any potential issues.

Manual Cleaning

Cleaning in addition to what is done naturally by rain will boost panel output, since on average they will have even less dirt on them.

Maintenance Consultancy

Our team of expert professionals is always here to provide you a consultation session, briefing you for what should be done to keep your solar panels alive & kicking!
